What is an Armstrong Number?

An Armstrong number is a number that equals the sum of its digits raised to the power of the number of digits.

  • 153 = 1³ + 5³ + 3³
  • 370 = 3³ + 7³ + 0³
  • 1634 = 1⁴ + 6⁴ + 3⁴ + 4⁴
