What is an Armstrong Number?

An Armstrong number (also called a narcissistic number) is a number that equals the sum of its own digits, each raised to the power of the total number of digits.

153

1³ + 5³ + 3³ = 153

370

3³ + 7³ + 0³ = 370

1634

1⁴ + 6⁴ + 3⁴ + 4⁴ = 1634
